About

Independent software consulting by Richard L Zarth III

Folklore Software LLC exists to help teams build, modernize, and rescue software with senior engineering judgment, clear communication, and a bias toward maintainable systems.

Folklore Software LLC

A practical consulting practice for teams that need experienced help

Folklore Software is strongest in iOS and Apple-platform work, especially apps that need modernization, architecture direction, product-minded delivery, backend integration, or help crossing from idea to production.

The best fit is software with enough backend, web, AI, or architecture complexity that a narrow handoff would miss what actually needs solving.

The working style is direct and collaborative: understand the problem, make the tradeoffs visible, ship useful increments, and leave the system easier to work with than it was before.

Working Style

Senior judgment without unnecessary process

Folklore Software is intentionally small, so clients work directly with the person doing the engineering, planning, and technical advising.

Clear communication

Practical updates, honest tradeoffs, and enough context for product owners and engineering teams to make good decisions.

Maintainable systems

Implementation that respects existing constraints while moving the codebase toward better structure and lower risk.

Teaching mindset

Consulting, pairing, review, and workshops that help teams understand the work rather than just receive a handoff.
